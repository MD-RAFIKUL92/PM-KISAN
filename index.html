PM-KISSAN Scheme

  •     PM Kisan is a Central Sector scheme with 100% funding from Government .
  •     It has become operational from 1.12.2018.
  •     Under the scheme an income support of 6,000/- per year in three equal .
  •     Definition of family for the scheme is husband, wife and minor children
  •     State Government and UT administration will identify the farmer families
  •     The fund will be directly transferred to the bank accounts of the .
